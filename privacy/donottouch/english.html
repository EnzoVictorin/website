Privacy Policy of
Don't Touch Your Screen

Protecting your privacy is very important to me, that’s why I do everything I can to minimize the data collected. The application will show you ads via the Google Mobile Ads SDK. The latter collects:

  • Your IP address, which is used to estimate the general location of the device.
  • Crash reports, which are used to improve the Google Mobile Ads SDK. This information can be used for advertising purposes.
  • Performance reports like:
    • Application launch time
    • Hang rate
    • Energy usage
    This data can be used to evaluate user behavior, understand the effectiveness of an existing feature and plan new ones. Performance reports can also be used to show you ads.
  • Ad performance reports that contain what the user has seen and information about interaction with the ads

This application does not track your activity on other applications and websites. You will not be shown any personalized ads. If you have purchased the in-app purchase to disable ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK will be disabled the next time you launch the application.
Your score is sent to Game Center to place you in a ranking that all other players can see. This score is associated with a nickname you chose when you set up Game Center. The developer does not collect any other data.
